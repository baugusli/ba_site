My Personality

Problem Solver

I get happy when I encounter a new problem. Because, with new problem I get to think hard and learn how to solve it. Furthermore, the reward for solving a problem is getting that sense of achievement, which is the best feeling in the world. The harder the problem is, the happier I get.

Swift Learner

I love to learn and experience new things. Sometimes the reason I want to learn new things is purely out of curiosity, sometimes because I want to achieve my goal and many times, because it's fun. Fortunately, I am blessed with the ability to pick up new things fast, and thanks to the Internet, I am getting new knowledge everyday.

Multi Cultural

With experience of living in three different countries, I have a multi cultural background. Being exposed to many different culture allows me to gain valuable knowledge regarding the world.

Perfectionist

From small requests by friends to important tasks at work, I always did them with by best ability. When applicable, I would take the initiative to go above and beyond. I learnt from past experiences that even a small mistake could lead to a huge disaster. Thus, I constantly train myself to do everything perfectly without a single mistake.

Observant

I am always curious about everything. Thus, whenever appropriate, I like to observe anything that intrigue me. Aftewards, I would ask myself: What happened? Why did that happened? How? Who is that?

Planner

Planning has play a critical role in my life. I plan on daily basis from what time I need to wakeup to allocating time for important assignments. As the result, I never miss a single deadline and are able to constantly produce great work efficiently.
